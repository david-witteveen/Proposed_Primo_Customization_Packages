How does Library Search work?

Library Search provides simple, one-stop searching for:

  • books
  • e-books
  • videos
  • articles and more

Library Search also helps you to manage your research. Sign-in to:

  • Renew books and other resources
  • Create favorites lists
  • Export citations to Endnote or your citation manager
